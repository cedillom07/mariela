About

My journey as a first-generation Latina has shaped my life in very positive ways by witnessing that with hard work and dedication so much is possible. Because of this, I hope that I accomplish my pursuit in representing my culture and paving the way for many to have and pursue their goals.

I'm Mariela Cedillo, and I've recently graduated from The University of Texas at Dallas. At UT Dallas, I was able to balance work and school life, even working two jobs at one point: my internship & my job at a restaurant. I worked at the restaurant for more than four years and it's taught me real-life lessons on how to interact with diverse people coming from different backgrounds, ages, and outlooks. At my internship with Ambit Energy, I was given the opportunity to work my first corporate job and was lucky enough to be a part of the IT Finance team while also working with the Sales, Japan, and Billing team. It was here that I gained a new perspective on the way each role in an IT team come together as one. Now, in 2020, I've become a part of a Fortune 500 utility company based here in Texas as a system engineer. In the near future, I hope to share my experiences with high school and college students by tutoring them in math courses. I've also earned a Spanish minor due to my interest in helping the Hispanic community with translation services. Below are some facts about me, and please don't hesitate to shoot an email! :)
